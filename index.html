UX (User Experience Design) is the interaction between the customer and the product that improves usability, ease of use and enjoyment by interacting with customers and products. From the very beginning, product development takes into account the principles of user interface (UI) design.


This is incredibly important, as few people want to use a product that is unattractive or difficult to navigate, especially in a new environment like the web or mobile phone.

Successful candidates will prove that they can provide adaptive and creative solutions to UI / UX design problems while keeping abreast of the latest design and technology trends. They will ensure that every element of the online user experience is optimized for improved user engagement, ease of use and overall user satisfaction.

Full stack developer

A full-stack developer is an engineer who takes over the work of database, server and system development for customers. They take the primary responsibility to develop complex software applications from scratch, and they know how to structure code, categorize files, structure data in databases and perform required computational tasks. Full-stack developers have a wide range of frontend and backend development capabilities

Salaries for web software engineers vary depending on what kind of web developer you want to be. When we talk about this, the average salary of a full-stack web developer is $93,402. Considering that a full web developer has all the responsibilities of a front-end and back-end developer, this salary is higher than others.


Companies like to hire developers with multidisciplinary skills, and this versatility is a major reason why cross-functional and full-stack developers are attractive to the recruitment pool. Full-stack developers are especially useful for companies with thin resources that cannot justify a separate front-end team, which is why many large companies have separate departments for their developers that need to be back-end-specific professionals. Developers who focus on both front-end and back-end development often overlap with the skills required for web development specializations.

A full-scale developer has the ability to work on both the front end and the back end of an application, software or website. To be clear, this means that a fullstack developer is a developer who uses a particular batch of technologies to create a fullstack application. Full-stack developers are considered jacks of all trades, not masters of all trades.

A Full-Stack developer offers unprecedented freedom to work on front-end and back-end development and in real time evaluate your website's capabilities and potential without waiting for another developer to check whether or not you want to do something as quickly as possible. Being a full-time developer is like being the key to a house whose door you can't open.
